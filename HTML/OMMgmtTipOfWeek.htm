Article Feedback & Ideas

All manuscripts should be sent via one of the two delivery mechanisms:

1. Electronically: Microsoft Word documents for text, images see note. All correspondence to: Jim Thomas at james.thomas@wolterskluwer.com. Note: if the author wishes to submit graphics electronically, an FTP site address will be provided to the author for transfer of high-resolution images.

2. Via mail or shipping service: CD-ROM should contain Microsoft Word documents for text, high-resolution .jpg or .tif files for graphics. All correspondence to: Jack Persico; Wolters Kluwer VisionCare Group, 323 Norristown Road, Suite 200, Ambler, PA 19002; Attn: Retinal Physician; phone: 215-628-7748. Manuscripts should be double-spaced, and fully referenced. Title page should include author affiliations, financial disclosure, and contact information.

References should be cited as per the AMA Manual of Style.

All figures must be referenced within the text, accompanied by complete legends, and be either free from copyright or, if previously published, accompanied by the proper permissions or contact information to obtain those permissions.

All graphics must meet the following criteria to be printed in Retinal Physician: 266 dpi @ 8" x 11".

Conflict-of-Interest Statement
With each submission, Retinal Physician requires that authors list financial relationships (including but not limited to employment, contract work, stock ownership, honoraria, paid testimony) or other conflicts of interest that would jeopardize the credibility of works published in Retinal Physician.

Statement of Informed Consent
Retinal Physician does not publish identifying information, including patient names, initials, or hospital numbers, unless the information is essential for scientific purposes and the patient (or parent or guardian) gives written informed consent for publication.
