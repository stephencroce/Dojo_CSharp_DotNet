New OD  in Print

Wouldn't you like to be able to tap the experience of both successful young ODs and optometry’s most accomplished practitioners as you face the real-world challenges that come after graduation? You can. New OD is a career- and practice-planning special section published within select issues of Optometric Management throughout the year. Look for it in your copies of Optometric Management as well as archived here on the New OD web page.
